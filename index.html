Join Us

We are looking for high-quality locally made jewelry to consign at Adorned. We are asking each artist to complete an application and submit a representative sample of the work they wish to put in the store for consideration to a jury of the owners for review.

Items can range from $15 to $5000 dollars. We will seriously consider all submissions, and will try to provide useful feedback if we find we are unable to accept your work. Originality of design and quality of material and finish will be critical to a positive outcome. If you are interested in joining us please complete the Adorned Consignment Form. We will begin scheduling our reviews as of March 1.
